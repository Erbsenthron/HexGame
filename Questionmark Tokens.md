These Tokens are placed by [[Dungeon Card|Dungeon Cards]] onto [[Tile|Dungeon Tiles]] to mark where players can interact with things in rooms. They are activated when a Player steps onto them, revealing what is underneath.
The token can contain:
	[[Traps]]
	Chests containing (Draw 2 keep 1 from [[Loot Card|Loot Deck]])
	Chest containing (Draw 3 keep 2 from [[Loot Card|Loot Deck]])
	[[Oracle Statue|Oracle Statues]]
