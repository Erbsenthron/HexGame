A Tile is hexagonal, with each border containing five half hexagons, that are walls. The center of each border is a door.

Colors: Blue, Yellow, Red, Purple
Each Tile has four types of colored Dots on it. Each color exists at least twice, with two of each type being numbered I and II.
When a Tile is uncovered, a Card is drawn that specifies, what is placed on each color of dot. A card MAY specify special rules for any numbered dots of a tile, for exaple: "Blue I. contains a chest requiring a gold key"
