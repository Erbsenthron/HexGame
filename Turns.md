Phases:
1. Regain Phase
	Players draw an [[Event Cards|Event card]]
	Players regain their [[Game Material|material]] as defined by their [[Loot Card|Items]]/[[Player Characters|Tableau]]
2. Fast Phase
	Player may take any fast actions they can
3. Enemy Fast Phase
	Fast/Mixed Enemies take their actions
4. Slow Phase
	Player may take any slow action they can
5. Enemy Slow Phase
	Slow/Mixed Enemies take their actions
