[[Turns|Turn order]]

Each Player gets a Tableau with their character description and starting stats like Health, how many Movement tokens to gain and the character actions they can take.

Token Types:
Movement Token: Double sided. Fast side, slow side. Fast Movement Tokens can be spent in both phases, slow Tokens only in the slow Phase
Health Token: When a Player has no more Health tokens, they go unconcious. A Player can spend x+y Energy, to revive an unconcious Player in an adjacient Space with that y+1 Health. x is specified on the player tableau.
Energy Tokens: A player is awarded a certain amount of Energy Tokens in each regain phase, that they may choose to spend on actions in either player phase. Unspent tokens are lost at the end of a turn.

Character Tableau:
How many fast/slow [[Movement Tokens]] does the Player receive in the Regain Phase
How many [[Health Tokens]] can the Player have/do they start with
How many [[Energy Tokens]] does the Player regain in the Regain Phase
How many [[Spells]], [[Weapons]], [[Armor|Armor Pieces]] of varying Types can the Character use
How many Items can the Player carry in their Backpack (Hand Cards)
Any intrinsic Abilities the character possesses
