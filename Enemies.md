Enemies decide who to attack like this:
Aggression effects (player more/less likely to be attacked)(From Curses more powerful than from Items) > Player that is nearest to the Enemy > Player with most Health > Character with the lowest Number on their Character Sheet

Enemy Health Works in Thresholds like this:
An Enemy might have Thresholds like 7/5/3
	If an attack does 1 damage, the enemy takes no damage
	If an Attack does 2 damage, the enemy moves to the 5 threshold
	If an Attack does 5 Damage, the enemy would have 2 health remaining, and moves to the 3 threshold.
	If an Attack does >=7 Damage, the enemy dies outright
	If the Enemy is at threshold 3 and an Attack does 3 or more damage, The Enemy dies


Enemy Attacks:
Enemies can be of three types: Fast, Slow, Mixed
Fast Enemies have a single attack, that the make in the [[Turns|Fast Enemy Phase]]
Slow Enemies have a single attack, that the make in the [[Turns|Slow Enemy Phase]]
Mixed Enemies have two Attacks, one fast, one slow, and thus attack in both [[Turns|Enemy Phases]]
