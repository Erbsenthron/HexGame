Traps often deal Damage or have other adverse effect on Players and or enemies within their range. These are the Traps that can be encountered:

Gas Trap: Deal ? Damage to all Players/Enemies within 1 Space of the Trap
Explosive Trap: Deal ? Damage to all Players/Enemies within 2 Spaces of the Trap. The Trap is then removed from the Board
Tripwire: The Player triggering this Trap loses all remaining Movement Tokens for this Turn. If Triggered by an Enemy, they cannot move any further for this turn. The Trap is then removed from the Board
